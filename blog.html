Upcoming Event: Student Orientation

We are proud to host an orientation on "Cleaning the World," an initiative dedicated to environmental sustainability and global stewardship. This event aims to educate and inspire students, faculty, and community members to take actionable steps towards a cleaner, greener planet. The orientation will feature keynote speakers from leading environmental organizations, interactive workshops on waste management and conservation practices, and collaborative projects to implement sustainable solutions both on campus and in the local community. Join us in this important effort to make a positive impact on our environment and future generations.

Annual awards function

Every year, our university hosts the prestigious Annual Awards Function, a night dedicated to celebrating the exceptional achievements of our students, faculty, and staff. This grand event brings together the entire campus community to honor academic excellence, groundbreaking research, community service, and outstanding leadership. Highlights of the evening include inspiring speeches, captivating performances, and the presentation of various awards and scholarships. The Annual Awards Function not only recognizes individual accomplishments but also reinforces our commitment to fostering a culture of excellence and innovation. Join us in celebrating the bright minds and dedicated hearts that make our university extraordinary.
