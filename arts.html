ARTS AND SCIENCE DEPARTMENT

In our college, undergraduate courses offer a rich tapestry of academic opportunities across various disciplines. Whether pursuing a Bachelor of Arts or Science, students delve into a curriculum designed to foster critical thinking, creativity, and practical skills. Engaging lectures, interactive seminars, and hands-on laboratory sessions form the backbone of our educational approach, supported by dedicated faculty committed to nurturing intellectual growth and personal development. With a focus on interdisciplinary learning and real-world applications, our undergraduate programs empower students to explore their passions, cultivate expertise, and embark on paths toward future success in their chosen fields.




FOREIGN RELATIONS


The Foreign Relations Studies program offers a comprehensive and interdisciplinary approach to understanding global affairs and international diplomacy. Students are immersed in a curriculum that covers political science, economics, history, and cultural studies, providing them with a deep understanding of the complexities of international relations. The program emphasizes critical thinking, analytical skills, and practical experiences.



Students have opportunities for internships with governmental and non-governmental organizations, study abroad programs, and participation in international conferences. Our distinguished faculty, with their extensive expertise and global networks, guide students through the intricacies of foreign policy, international law, and global governance.


Graduates of the program are well-prepared for careers in diplomacy, international organizations, global business, and academia. They make significant contributions to the advancement of peaceful and cooperative international relations, equipped with the knowledge and skills gained from our rigorous and dynamic program.



BIOLOGICAL SCIENCES



Our Biological Sciences department offers a comprehensive education covering genetics, ecology, microbiology, and biochemistry. Students benefit from hands-on laboratory work and field experiences, fostering strong scientific inquiry and critical thinking skills essential for careers in research, healthcare, conservation, and biotechnology.


Our faculty are dedicated mentors and researchers, actively involving students in cutting-edge projects. Small class sizes promote personalized learning and collaborative environments where students explore their scientific interests and pursue research initiatives.


Graduates of our Biological Sciences program emerge with a solid foundation in biological principles and practical skills that are highly valued in both academic and industry settings. They are equipped to tackle global challenges in health, environmental sustainability, and biotechnological innovation, making meaningful contributions to society through their knowledge and expertise.



PSYCHOLOGICAL SCIENCES


our Psychological Sciences department offers a comprehensive education in understanding human behavior and mental processes. Our curriculum blends theoretical knowledge with practical applications, preparing students for careers in clinical practice, education, research, and organizational psychology.



Students in our program engage in experiential learning opportunities, including internships, research projects, and community service initiatives, which enhance their analytical and interpersonal skills. Our faculty members are accomplished scholars and practitioners who mentor students in both theoretical knowledge and practical applications, preparing them for successful careers in psychology and related fields.


Graduates of our Psychological Sciences program emerge with a solid foundation in psychological theories, research methodologies, and ethical practices. They are equipped to address real-world challenges, promote mental health and well-being, and contribute positively to society through their expertise and compassion.



GEOLOGICAL SCIENCES


the Geological Sciences program offers students an in-depth exploration of the Earth's processes, history, and resources. Our curriculum integrates fieldwork, laboratory studies, and theoretical coursework, providing a comprehensive understanding of geology, mineralogy, paleontology, and environmental geosciences.



Students engage in hands-on learning experiences, conducting field expeditions to study geological formations firsthand and analyze geological data. Our faculty, comprised of leading researchers and experts in the field, mentor students in cutting-edge research initiatives and provide guidance in understanding geological phenomena and their global impacts.


Graduates of our Geological Sciences program are well-prepared for careers in natural resource management, environmental consulting, geological exploration, and academia. They contribute to society through their expertise in understanding Earth's systems, addressing environmental challenges, and promoting sustainable practices.



FARM SCIENCES



the Farm Sciences program is dedicated to advancing agricultural knowledge and practices. Our curriculum covers crop science, soil science, animal husbandry, and sustainable agriculture, combining classroom instruction with hands-on farm experience to equip students with practical skills and scientific understanding.


Students participate in fieldwork, laboratory research, and internships, gaining insights into modern farming techniques and sustainable practices. Our experienced faculty guide students through innovative research projects and provide mentorship in the latest agricultural technologies and methodologies.


Graduates of our Farm Sciences program are well-prepared for careers in agriculture, agribusiness, environmental conservation, and research. They contribute to the agricultural industry by promoting sustainable farming practices and addressing global food security challenges.
